The meeting was called to order at 5.05 p.m.
Adoption of the agenda
The agenda was adopted.
The situation in the Middle East
Report of the Secretary-General on the United
Nations Interim Force in Lebanon (S/2002/746)
The President: On behalf of the Council, I
welcome the presence of the Secretary-General at this
meeting and the one to follow. It is very good that he
can join us for these mandate renewals.
The Security Council will now begin its
consideration of the item on its agenda. The Council is
meeting in accordance with the understanding reached
in its prior consultations.
Members of the Council have before them the
report of the Secretary-General on the United Nations
Interim Force in Lebanon, document S/2002/746.
Members of the Council also have before them
document S/2002/846, which contains the text of a
draft resolution submitted by France.
I should also like to draw the attention of
members of the Council to the following documents:
S/2002/739, letter dated 9 July 2002 from the
representative of Lebanon addressed to the Secretary-
General, and S/2002/806, letter dated 22 July 2002
from the representative of Lebanon addressed to the
Secretary-General.
It is my understanding that the Council is ready to
proceed to the vote of the draft resolution before it.
Unless I hear any objection, I shall put the draft
resolution to the vote now.
There being no objection, it is so decided.
A vote was taken by show of hands.
In favour:
Bulgaria, Cameroon, China, Colombia, France,
Guinea, Ireland, Mauritius, Mexico, Norway,
Russian Federation, Singapore, Syrian Arab
Republic, United Kingdom of Great Britain and
Northern Ireland, United States of America
The President: The draft resolution received 15
votes in favour. The draft resolution has been adopted
unanimously as resolution 1428 (2002).
The Security Council has thus concluded the
present stage of its consideration of the item on its
agenda.
The meeting rose at 5.10 p.m.
