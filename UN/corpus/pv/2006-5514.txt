Minute of silence
The President: Tomorrow, Saturday, will be the
third anniversary of the bombing of United Nations
Headquarters in Baghdad, in which 22 people lost their
lives and hundreds more were injured on 19 August
2003. May I ask the members of the Security Council
to join me in observing a minute of silence to honour
the memory of those who were killed while assisting
the Iraqi people in their pursuit of peace and stability?
The members of the Security Council observed a
minute of silence.
Adoption of the agenda
The agenda was adopted.
