Resolution 1469 (2003)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 4725th meeting,
on 25 March 2003
The Security Council,
Reaffirming all its previous resolutions on Western Sahara, in particular
resolution 1429 (2002) of 30 July 2002,
1. Decides to extend the mandate of the United Nations Mission for the
Referendum in Western Sahara (MINURSO) until 31 May 2003;
2. Requests the Secretary-General to provide a report on the situation by
19 May 2003 as proposed by the Secretary-General in his letter to the President of
the Security Council of 19 March 2003 (S/2003/341);
3. Decides to remain seized of the matter.
