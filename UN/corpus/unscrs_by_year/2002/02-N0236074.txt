Resolution 1406 (2002)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 4523rd meeting, on
30 April 2002
The Security Council,
Recalling all its previous resolutions on Western Sahara, in particular
resolution 1394 (2002) of 27 February 2002,
1. Decides to extend the mandate of the United Nations Mission for the
Referendum in Western Sahara (MINURSO) until 31 July 2002 in order to consider
further the report of the Secretary-General of 19 February 2002 (S/2002/178);
2. Decides to remain seized of the matter.
