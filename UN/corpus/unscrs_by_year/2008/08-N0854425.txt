Resolution 1839 (2008)
Adopted by the Security Council at its 5992nd meeting, on
9 October 2008
The Security Council,
Recalling all its relevant resolutions including resolution 1808 of 15 April
2008 (S/RES/1808),
Taking note of the reports of the Secretary-General of 23 July 2008
(S/2008/480) and 3 October 2008 (S/2008/631),
1. Decides to extend the mandate of the United Nations mission for a new
period terminating on 15 February 2009;
2. Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
